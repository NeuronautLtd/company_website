Neuronaut Ltd

Neuronaut Ltd was founded to share expertise in computational neuroscience and to provide innovative solutions for clients. Our services evolved from a solid academic background and a passion for the intersection of neuroscience, emotions, and computing.

Neuroscience consultancy

We offer professional consultancy services that focus on the latest advancements in computational principles in neuroscience. With a core fascination in affective neuroscience and psychiatry, we provide valuable insights and guidance to help clients navigate the complex landscape of brain research and its practical applications.

Data analytics

Utilizing a strong background in data analysis, we assist clients in extracting meaningful information from complex data sets. By applying advanced statistical and computational methods, we help clients understand the underlying patterns and relationships in their data, enabling them to make informed decisions and uncover new opportunities.

Digital tools

We develop and implement digital tools designed to enhance clients' understanding of neural processes and optimize their research endeavors. Drawing from our experience in app development and computational neuroscience, we create customized software solutions that streamline data processing, improve research methodologies, and facilitate the exploration of new ideas.
